We’ve just shipped **KindFi News**—your new home for platform updates, product releases, and transparent stories from the projects and causes listed on KindFi.  
It’s fast, simple, and built to help more people discover verifiable impact across LATAM.

- Visit the hub: **[KindFi News](/news)**
- Discover active campaigns: **[Explore Projects](/projects)**

## What’s included today

- **Platform updates & release notes**  
  Roadmaps, UI improvements, and behind-the-scenes changes that make KindFi better.

- **Project & cause posts**  
  Listed projects can publish **impact stories, milestone reports, and updates** directly on KindFi—no extra tools required.

- **Proof-friendly content**  
  Posts can include references to **milestone-based escrow** and on-chain activity to back up claims with verifiable data.

<Callout type="info" title="Transparent by design">
  KindFi integrates **escrow on Stellar**, enabling projects to report
  milestones with links to on-chain references for donors to verify.
</Callout>

## For projects: publish your first post

1. Go to your project page and open **Updates**.
2. Click **New Post**, add a clear title, a 1–2 sentence summary, and a cover image.
3. Tag it by **category** (e.g., education, clean water) and **region**.
4. Reference the **milestone** you’re reporting on—include any relevant on-chain links.

<Callout type="success" title="Tip for better reach">
  Use specific, outcome-focused titles (e.g., “50 filters installed in
  Puntarenas, Costa Rica”) and include costs, photos, and verification details.
</Callout>

## For donors and supporters

- **Follow along in one place**: Read all updates on **[KindFi News](/news)** or on each project’s **Updates** tab.
- **Subscribe via RSS**: Add **/news/rss** to your reader to get new posts.
- **Share impact stories**: Posts include rich previews for X, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp.

## Content you’ll see often

- **Impact stories**: People helped, before/after, and measurable outcomes.
- **Milestone reports**: What was delivered, costs, and links to escrow releases.
- **Product notes**: Small quality-of-life improvements that make KindFi faster and easier.
- **Partner spotlights**: Collaboration across the Stellar ecosystem to unlock more good.
