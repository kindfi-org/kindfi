On **June 24, 2026**, powerful earthquakes struck **Venezuela**, leaving families and communities facing damaged homes, limited access to clean water, food, medical care, and other essentials. KindFi is launching a dedicated campaign so anyone, anywhere, can contribute through a **transparent, secure donation experience**.

- **Support the campaign:** [Venezuela Earthquake Emergency Relief](/projects/venezuela-earthquake-emergency-relief)
- **Learn about the relief fund:** [DECAF Venezuela Earthquake Relief](https://www.decaf.so/en/venezuela-earthquake-relief)

## Why we launched this campaign

When disasters hit, speed and trust matter. Donors want to know that their contribution reaches people who need it—not weeks later, and not through opaque channels.

KindFi created **[Venezuela Earthquake Emergency Relief](/projects/venezuela-earthquake-emergency-relief)** to make cross-border giving straightforward:

- **On-chain escrow** secures every donation on the Stellar network until funds are released according to the campaign plan.
- **Verifiable project page** with milestones, updates, and an auditable escrow contract on Stellar Explorer.
- **Direct path to trusted relief** — funds raised through this campaign are forwarded to **DECAF's official Venezuela Earthquake Relief wallet**, which supports verified organizations delivering aid on the ground.

<Callout type="info" title="Where your donation goes">
  Funds from this KindFi campaign are sent to DECAF's dedicated relief fund. DECAF
  coordinates support through **Dividendo Voluntario para la Comunidad (DVC)**, a
  Venezuelan nonprofit with local presence and experience mobilizing resources inside
  Venezuela. Learn more on the [DECAF relief page](https://www.decaf.so/en/venezuela-earthquake-relief).
</Callout>

## What communities need right now

Survivors across affected regions are facing urgent gaps in basic services. Relief efforts focus on delivering:

- **Clean water and food** for displaced families
- **Medicines and medical supplies** where clinics are overwhelmed
- **Shelter materials and emergency kits** for damaged homes
- **Hygiene and basic household supplies** to prevent secondary health crises

Every contribution strengthens the collective response. Small donations add up quickly when thousands of people act together.

## How KindFi keeps giving transparent

Unlike traditional crowdfunding, KindFi campaigns are built around **milestone-based escrow** powered by [Trustless Work](https://trustlesswork.com) on Stellar:

1. **You donate** through the project page — connect a wallet or follow the campaign's donation flow.
2. **Funds are secured** in an on-chain escrow contract you can inspect on Stellar Explorer.
3. **Releases follow the plan** — milestones and updates show what was promised, what was delivered, and when funds moved.
4. **The project publishes updates** on KindFi so supporters can follow progress without leaving the platform.

This is the same infrastructure KindFi uses across LATAM for education, clean water, and community development campaigns—now applied to **rapid humanitarian response**.
