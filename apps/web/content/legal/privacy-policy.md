# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

KindFi respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

- Account information
- Wallet addresses
- Passkey authentication metadata
- KYC verification status through third-party providers
- Donation and campaign activity
- Device and analytics information

## How We Use Information

- Operate the platform
- Prevent fraud
- Meet legal obligations
- Improve the service

## Blockchain

Blockchain transactions are public and immutable. Wallet addresses and transaction history cannot be altered once recorded.

## Third Parties

We use external providers for KYC, hosting, analytics, and wallet connectivity.

## Contact

Questions may be sent through https://www.kindfi.org.
