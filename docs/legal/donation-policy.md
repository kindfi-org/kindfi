# Donation Policy

All donations are voluntary.

Donations are generally **non-refundable**.

Funds are held in milestone-based smart contract escrows.

If milestones are completed, funds are released according to the campaign.

If a campaign fails, unreleased funds are transferred to the **KindFi Community Fund (Community Vault)**.

Funds are **not refunded** to donors.

Once each year, eligible governance participants vote on how Community Fund assets are redistributed to qualified impact projects.
